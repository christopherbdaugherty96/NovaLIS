What Nova Actually Is
Nova is not just a chatbot.

It is a personal intelligence workspace with explicit memory, project continuity, trust review, bounded second-opinion reasoning, and an early home-agent layer that still answers to you.

A Real User Flow

This is the current Nova experience in one short loop.

You: "Explain this error"
Nova: Breaks it down and gives you the likely cause.

You: "Second opinion and final answer"
Nova: Runs a bounded review, summarizes the gap, and gives you a stronger final answer.

You: "Remember this fix"
Nova: Saves it because you explicitly chose to keep it.
