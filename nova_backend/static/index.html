    Introduction

    Welcome to Nova

    Offline-first

    Nova helps you understand information, continue your work, and make decisions without ever taking control away from you.

    No background automation. No hidden memory. No surprises. Just intelligence under your control.

    Local Mode: Nova stays local-first, private, and cost-free by default.

    What You Can Do

    Start simple. Nova becomes useful fast.

    • Research: Ask deeper questions than search alone.
    • Explain: Understand a file, page, result, or something on your screen.
    • Continue: Pick up a project or thread where you left off.
    • Save: Preserve only the facts or decisions you explicitly choose.
    • Control: Use simple governed system actions when they are explicitly allowed.
    Why Nova Feels Different

    Nova is powerful because it stays bounded and visible.

    It Waits For You
    Nothing runs unless you ask for it or approve it.
    It Shows Its Work
    You can inspect actions, sources, settings, and recent activity.
    It Stays Bounded
    Nova can reason broadly without quietly turning into an autonomous agent.
    A Simple Moment

    Here is what Nova feels like in practice.

    You: "Explain this error" -> Nova: cause + next step -> You: "Save this as my fix"

    Nova helps with the answer, and it only preserves something when you explicitly ask it to. That is the loop: Understand -> Continue -> Preserve -> Decide.

    Setup Options

    You can stay fully local or choose a future cloud path later in Settings.

    Local Mode
    Uses local AI models, needs no API key, and is the safest current everyday setup.
    Bring Your Own API Key
    Lets you control provider, usage, and billing once you explicitly connect one.
    Managed Cloud Access
    Will be the easiest future setup path, but should still stay visible and explicit.
    Start Here

    You can be useful in a minute.

    • Start with news, explain this, or continue my project.
    • Open Settings any time to change setup mode, permissions, accessibility, or voice preferences.
    • Open Workspace when you want your ongoing work, recent decisions, and structure map together.
    • Open Trust when you want to see what happened, what stayed blocked, and why.
    • Nova does not act, save, or spend without your request.

    Intelligence can grow. Control stays with you.
