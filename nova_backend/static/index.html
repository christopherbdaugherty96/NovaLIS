About Nova

Nova is a local-first assistant that helps you find information, understand topics, and control simple things on your system when you ask.

It can search the web, summarize news or research topics, open websites or files, and provide structured explanations with sources and confidence indicators. Nova also offers a daily brief with weather, headlines, and system information so you can quickly see what is happening.

Nova is designed to stay predictable and under your control. It does not act on its own, does not run tasks in the background without being asked, and shows when information comes from the internet or when something is uncertain.

Tip: Try asking "show today's brief" or "search for today's tech news."
