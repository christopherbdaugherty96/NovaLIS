\# NovaLIS Phase-3 Lock (GOVERNED EXPANSION — FUTURE)



Phase-3 is not active until Phase-2 exit criteria are satisfied and documentation is frozen.



Theme: Governed expansion while preserving the governor model and explicit control.



---



\## Phase-3 Allowed (Only after explicit unlock)

\- Carefully scoped expansion of skills or integrations

\- Additional executor targets (laptop/phone clients) under the trusted capability model

\- Optional, explicit “Reasoning / Reflective Mode” improvements (still user-invoked, bounded)

\- Improved documentation, testing, and reliability tooling (non-autonomous)



---



\## Phase-3 Forbidden (Still forbidden)

\- Autonomous agents

\- Background monitoring or proactive behavior

\- Self-optimizing loops

\- Implicit personalization / learning

\- LLM-driven routing or actions



---



\## Phase-3 Entry Criteria

\- Phase-2 complete and stable end-to-end

\- Acceptance tests exist and pass reliably

\- Logs and correlation IDs are consistent

\- No duplicate frontend/backend sources-of-truth



---



\## Phase-3 Exit Criteria

\- Expansion does not violate Phase-1/2 contracts

\- System remains auditable and predictable

\- All new capabilities are documented with explicit contracts
