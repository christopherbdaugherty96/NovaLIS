# Paper Wallet

Defines simulated trading behavior for Market Sandbox.

---

## Purpose

Provide safe learning environment with no real money.

---

## Rules

- no real execution
- no broker API
- no account access

---

## Parameters

- starting balance
- max trade size
- daily loss limit
- allowed assets

---

## Simulation

- simulate entry/exit
- track open positions
- track PnL

---

## Output

- trade record
- journal entry

---

## Safety

- results are not guarantees

---

## Status

Planning only
