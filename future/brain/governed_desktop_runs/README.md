---

## Purpose

This area represents how NovaLIS may safely act in the future.

It defines:

- task-scoped execution
- governed envelopes
- approval-first execution
- scope monitoring
- stop conditions

---

## Relationship to Brain

The Brain:

- proposes the task
- builds the envelope
- explains the plan

The Governor:

- enforces the rules
- allows or denies execution

Execution:

- remains bounded
- remains logged
- remains interruptible

---

## Key Rule

> Planning happens in the brain. Authority remains outside the brain.
