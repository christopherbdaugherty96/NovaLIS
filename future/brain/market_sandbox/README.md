---

## Relationship to Brain

The Brain:

- analyzes data
- explains signals
- references past learning

Learning System:

- records outcomes
- suggests improvements
- does not grant authority

Governance:

- controls any real-world execution

---

## Key Rule

> Learning feeds the brain. Learning does not become authority.
