# Nova Product Messaging Variants
Updated: 2026-03-26

## Purpose
This guide keeps Nova's product-facing messaging in one place so we can reuse it across:
- a landing page
- the in-app onboarding flow
- investor and partner positioning

The goal is to keep Nova's message clear:
- powerful
- understandable
- governed
- under user control

## A. Landing Page Copy

### Hero
Nova is your personal intelligence system.

Understand. Continue. Decide.
Without giving up control.

No background automation.
No hidden memory.
No surprises.

Just intelligence, under your control.

### Why Nova
Most AI tools try to act for you.

Nova does not.

It helps you:
- understand what you are looking at
- continue your work
- organize important decisions

And it only moves when you tell it to.

### What You Can Do
- Research: Ask deeper questions than search alone.
- Explain: Point at anything and get clear answers.
- Continue: Pick up where you left off instantly.
- Save: Remember only what you choose.
- Control: Use simple governed actions on your system.

### The Difference
Other AI products often:
- act automatically
- remember implicitly
- hide what they are doing

Nova:
- waits for you
- shows what it used
- keeps you in control

### A Simple Moment
You:
`Explain this error`

Nova:
Breaks it down, explains the likely cause, and tells you what to try next.

You:
`Save this as my fix`

Now it is part of your workflow because you chose to keep it.

### Core Loop
Nova helps you:

`Understand -> Continue -> Preserve -> Decide`

### Get Started
Try:
- `news`
- `explain this`
- `continue my project`

Nova takes it from there.

## B. In-App Onboarding Copy

### Welcome to Nova
Nova helps you understand information, continue your work, and stay organized without ever acting on its own.

### How Nova Works
- Nothing runs unless you ask.
- Nothing is saved unless you choose.
- Nothing happens in the background.

Nova is built to feel predictable, calm, and under your control.

### What You Can Do
- Research: Ask questions and explore topics.
- Explain: Understand files, pages, results, or things on your screen.
- Continue: Resume a project, thread, or line of thinking.
- Save: Keep important facts or decisions explicitly.

### Try This First
Start simple:
- `news`
- `more on story 1`
- `explain this`
- `continue my project`

### Memory
Nova does not remember automatically.

You control memory with explicit commands such as:
- `remember this`
- `list memories`
- `show memory <id>`

### Workflow
Nova is designed around one simple loop:

`Understand -> Continue -> Preserve -> Decide`

### Ready
Start typing whenever you are ready.

## C. Investor / Positioning Copy

### Nova
Nova is a governance-first personal intelligence platform.

It separates:
- intelligence
- from authority

That means Nova can grow smarter without quietly growing more powerful.

### Problem
Many AI systems:
- act without clear boundaries
- store data implicitly
- provide limited transparency
- create trust and control problems

### Solution
Nova takes a different path:
- all actions are explicitly invoked
- no background execution
- no implicit memory
- full visibility and auditability

### Core Capabilities
- structured research and analysis
- document and context explanation
- session and workspace continuity
- explicit governed memory
- governed system interaction

### Architecture Advantage
Nova enforces:
- a Governor layer for execution
- capability gating through the registry
- ledger-backed action visibility
- offline-first operation when possible

This creates a bounded intelligence system instead of an unconstrained autonomous agent.

### Strategic Position
Nova is not:
- a basic assistant
- an uncontrolled agent
- a hidden automation layer

Nova is:

`a governed personal intelligence layer`

### Market Direction
As AI grows more capable, the core product question changes from:

`Can AI do this?`

to:

`Can I trust it when it does?`

Nova answers that with:
- control
- transparency
- predictability

### Vision
Nova is growing toward a governed cognitive operating system for individuals first and broader networks later.
