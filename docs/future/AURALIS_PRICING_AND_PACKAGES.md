# Auralis Pricing and Packages

Date: 2026-04-26

Status: Skeleton / future planning

---

## Purpose

Define a realistic pricing ladder for Auralis web services and future Nova-backed add-ons.

Pricing should remain flexible until real demand, scope, and delivery time are validated.

---

## Core Principles

- Sell clear outcomes, not vague features
- Keep entry offers simple
- Avoid underpricing custom work
- Prefer recurring maintenance where value is real
- Do not charge for unsupported Nova features
- Price support burden into higher tiers
