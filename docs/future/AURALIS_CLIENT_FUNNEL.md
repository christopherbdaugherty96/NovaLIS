# Auralis Client Funnel

Date: 2026-04-26

Status: Skeleton / future planning

---

## Purpose

Define the path from prospect discovery to signed client, launch, retention, and upsell.

---

## Funnel Stages

1. Prospect discovery
2. Outreach / inbound inquiry
3. Qualification
4. Quote
5. Close
6. Build
7. Launch
8. Maintenance
9. Upsell to Nova-backed services
10. Referral / repeat business

---

## Stage Details

### 1. Prospect Discovery

Sources:
- Local businesses without websites
- Weak/outdated websites
- Referrals
- Social media inbound
- Networking

### 2. Outreach / Inbound Inquiry

Goal:
- Start conversation
- Identify need
- Book discovery call or gather scope

### 3. Qualification

Questions:
- What business type?
- Need website or redesign?
- Budget range?
- Timeline?
- Who decides?

### 4. Quote

Deliverables:
- Scope
- Price
- Timeline
- Payment terms
- Revision terms

### 5. Close

Actions:
- Collect deposit
- Confirm assets needed
- Schedule kickoff

### 6. Build

Actions:
- Gather content
- Build pages
- Review drafts
- Revise
- Prepare launch

### 7. Launch

Checklist:
- Domain / hosting
- Mobile check
- Contact forms working
- SEO basics
- Analytics installed if used

### 8. Maintenance

Ongoing:
- Updates
- Fixes
- Content changes
- Reviews

### 9. Upsell to Nova-backed Services

When appropriate:
- Lead summaries
- Draft replies
- Metrics reports
- Managed insights

### 10. Referral / Repeat Business

Ask for:
- Testimonial
- Referral
- Additional locations/services work
