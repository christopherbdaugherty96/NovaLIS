## Purpose

Define the path from prospect discovery to signed client, launch, retention, upsell, and referral. The goal is to turn random opportunities into a repeatable client acquisition system.

---

## Funnel Stages

1. Prospect discovery
2. Outreach / inbound inquiry
3. Qualification
4. Quote / proposal
5. Close / deposit
6. Onboarding
7. Build
8. Launch
9. Maintenance / retention
10. Upsell to Nova-backed services
11. Referral / repeat business

---

## Stage Details

## 1. Prospect Discovery

### Sources

- Local businesses without websites
- Weak or outdated websites
- Referrals
- Social media inbound
- Networking
- Existing customers with additional needs

### Goal

Create a steady list of qualified opportunities.

### Output

- Lead list
- Notes on opportunity type
- Contact path
- Priority level

---

## 2. Outreach / Inbound Inquiry

### Goal

Start conversation and identify the real need.

### Outreach Paths

- Personalized direct outreach
- Referral introduction
- Social media message
- Website inquiry form
- Phone call

### Output

- Discovery conversation booked
- Scope questions answered
- Lead marked warm / cold / follow-up

---

## 3. Qualification

### Core Questions

- What business type?
- Need first website or redesign?
- Main problem right now?
- Budget range?
- Timeline?
- Who decides?
- Need maintenance after launch?
- Need help with leads or follow-up?

### Goal

Avoid spending build time on poor-fit or non-serious leads.

### Output

- Qualified / not qualified
- Recommended package
- Notes for proposal

---

## 4. Quote / Proposal

### Deliverables

- Scope
- Deliverables
- Price
- Timeline
- Payment terms
- Revision terms
- Optional add-ons
- Expiration date if desired

### Goal

Make buying easy and clear.

### Output

- Proposal sent
- Follow-up date set

---

## 5. Close / Deposit

### Actions

- Collect deposit
- Confirm acceptance of scope
- Confirm timeline
- Schedule kickoff

### Goal

Turn opportunity into active project.

### Output

- Active client
- Deposit received
- Kickoff scheduled

---

## 6. Onboarding

### Collect

- Logo
- Brand colors
- Photos
- Service list
- Business details
- Social links
- Preferred contact info
- Testimonials
- Domain/hosting access if needed

### Goal

Get all required assets early to avoid delays.

### Output

- Ready-to-build project folder
- Missing asset checklist

---

## 7. Build

### Actions

- Build pages
- Add content
- Configure forms
- Mobile optimization
- Review draft with client
- Revise within scope
- Prepare launch checklist

### Goal

Deliver a site that looks professional and converts inquiries.

### Output

- Approved launch candidate

---

## 8. Launch

### Checklist

- Domain connected
- Hosting live
- SSL working if applicable
- Mobile check complete
- Forms tested
- Basic SEO complete
- Analytics installed if used
- Contact details verified

### Goal

Launch cleanly with no obvious trust-breaking issues.

### Output

- Live website
- Launch confirmation sent

---

## 9. Maintenance / Retention

### Ongoing Services

- Content edits
- Fixes
- Form testing
- Review updates
- SEO touchups
- Performance checks
- Strategy suggestions

### Goal

Retain customer and create recurring revenue.

### Output

- Active monthly relationship

---

## 10. Upsell to Nova-Backed Services

### When Appropriate

Use only when the customer has enough inquiry volume or workflow pain to justify it.

### Possible Offers

- Lead summaries
- Draft replies
- Metrics reports
- Follow-up suggestions
- Managed insights

### Goal

Increase value after trust is already established.

### Output

- Added recurring service tier

---

## 11. Referral / Repeat Business

### Ask For

- Testimonial
- Referral
- Additional services
- New location website
- Seasonal campaigns

### Goal

Lower future acquisition cost through trust and reputation.

### Output

- Referral pipeline
- Expansion revenue

---

## Metrics to Track

### Sales Metrics

- Leads per month
- Qualified leads per month
- Quote acceptance rate
- Average project value
- Time to close

### Delivery Metrics

- Average build time
- Revision rounds per project
- Launch delays caused by missing assets
- Post-launch issues found

### Revenue Metrics

- Monthly recurring revenue
- Maintenance attach rate
- Upsell conversion rate
- Referral revenue
