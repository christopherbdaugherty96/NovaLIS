# Auralis Risk and Policy

Date: 2026-04-26

Status: Skeleton / future planning

---

## Purpose

Define practical risk controls, customer expectations, and policy boundaries for future Auralis + Nova services.

---

## Core Principles

- Be honest about what exists today
- No hidden automation
- Customer stays in control of sensitive actions
- Minimize data collection
- Protect trust over growth speed
- Fail safely and visibly

---

## Customer Expectations

Customers should understand:

- What Auralis builds today
- What Nova features are live vs future
- What actions require approval
- What data is used
- What support is included
- What is not guaranteed
