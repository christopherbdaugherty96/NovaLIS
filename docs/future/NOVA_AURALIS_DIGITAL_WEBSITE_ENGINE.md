# Nova x Auralis Digital Website Engine

Status: future concept / business application

This document captures a future business-facing application of Nova: a governed website production engine for Auralis Digital.

It does not represent a shipped Nova runtime feature. It is a design and business plan for how Nova could help produce professional websites for real businesses while keeping actions visible, reviewable, and user-approved.

---

## Plain-English Summary

Auralis Digital can operate as a client-facing web services brand.
Nova can operate as the governed intelligence and workflow layer behind it.

The customer experience:

- client provides business info
- client uploads photos
- client selects preferred style
- Nova drafts a premium website
- human reviews and refines
- final site is delivered or published with approval

This combines service business practicality with Nova's governance model.

## Client Intake Inputs

A strong intake process should capture:

- business name
- industry
- city / service area
- services offered
- pricing model if relevant
- phone / email / contact info
- booking link if any
- social links
- photos / logo
- testimonials
- desired style
- competitors they like
- primary goal (calls, leads, bookings, orders)

Good intake quality improves final output quality.

---

## Website Workflow

1. Create client profile.
2. Collect photos, services, contact info, and goals.
3. Select style direction.
4. Nova proposes sitemap and sections.
5. Nova drafts copy and page structure.
6. Nova assembles website draft.
7. Human reviews design quality and conversion flow.
8. Client requests revisions.
9. Final approval.
10. Publish or hand off files.

The MVP should focus on draft production only.

Nova can safely generate:

- homepage draft
- services page draft

## Governance Rules

- No publishing live without approval.
- No domain purchases without approval.
- No client billing actions without approval.
- No false claims in copy.
- No hidden edits to live sites.
- No destructive changes without backup or confirmation.
- All external actions should be reviewable and logged.
