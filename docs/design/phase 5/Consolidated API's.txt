# 🌐 NovaLIS – Consolidated API & Integration Registry

This document merges all APIs and programmatic integrations from both the *Strategic API Integration* plan and the *Locked API Roadmap*. Only APIs that respect Nova’s governor‑first architecture are listed. Each entry includes a brief description and, where applicable, the approved phase from the locked roadmap.

---

## ☁️ Weather & Environment

- **Open‑Meteo** – Free global weather API, no key required. Current conditions & forecasts.  
- **WeatherAPI** – Free tier: current weather, forecast, astronomy, air quality.  
- **QWeather (HeWeather)** – Free global weather data with API key.  
- **Storm Glass** – Marine and coastal weather data.  
- **OpenUV** – Free UV index and related environmental data.  
- **Pirate Weather** – Free, privacy‑focused Dark Sky alternative (from earlier notes).  

---

## 📰 News & Structured Content

- **NewsData.io** – Global news headlines with filtering, ~200 requests/day free.  
- **Currents API** – News headlines, ~600 free requests/day; can be used for multi‑source verification.  
- **Guardian API** – Unlimited free access to The Guardian content.  
- **New York Times API** – Unlimited free article access.  
- **Wikipedia API** – Unlimited free, structured encyclopedia content.  
- **OpenAlex** – Free scholarly metadata for research and academic content.  

---

## 💰 Finance & Market Data

- **CoinGecko API** – Free cryptocurrency prices, market data; many endpoints no auth.  
- **ExchangeRate‑API / AbstractAPI Currency** – Currency conversion, free tiers.  
- **Finnhub.io** – Real‑time stock market data & indicators (free tier).  
- **Alpha Vantage** – Stock, FX, and crypto data (5 calls/min free) – *legacy, still viable*.  

---

## 🔔 Notifications & Messaging

- **Pushover** – Push notifications, free tier with monthly limit.  
- **ntfy.sh** – Simple, free push notifications via HTTP webhooks.  

---

## 📅 Calendar & Email (Read‑Only)

- **Google Calendar API** – Free, OAuth2, read‑only or write‑with‑approval (Phase 4.1+).  
- **CalDAV** – Open calendar standard (e.g., Nextcloud), read/write with approval.  
- **Microsoft Graph Calendar** – Outlook/Office365 scheduling, free tier.  
- **Gmail API / IMAP** – Read‑only email summaries, user‑invoked.  

---

## ☁️ Cloud Storage & File Management

- **Google Drive API** – 15 GB free, governed file ops (read/append, no delete without approval).  
- **Dropbox API** – Basic free tier, cloud file operations with reversibility.  

---

## 🔎 Fact Checking & Quick Answers

- **DuckDuckGo Instant Answer API** – Free, lightweight quick answers.  
- **Wolfram Alpha** – Computational knowledge and structured answers (limited free tier).  

---

## 🧠 AI / NLP & Reasoning

- **DeepSeek API** – Constitutional reasoning engine; multi‑perspective analysis, no autonomy.  
- **Hugging Face Inference API** – Free inference on community models (rate‑limited).  
- **OpenAI / Claude / AI grounding APIs** – Text, code, reasoning (limited free trial).  
- **Jina AI** – Document/PDF processing (free tier) – *legacy, still useful*.  
- **AssemblyAI** – Audio transcription/analysis (free tier) – *legacy*.  

---

## 🛠️ Utility & Enrichment APIs

- **AbstractAPI Utilities** – Geolocation, email validation, phone, etc. (free tier).  
- **REST Countries API** – Country info (population, codes, flags), no auth.  
- **Free Public APIs Directory** – Meta‑resource to discover current free APIs.  

---

## 🏠 Home Automation (Local APIs)

- **Home Assistant API** – Unified local control for smart devices.  
- **MQTT Broker** – Local IoT messaging protocol.  
- **Philips Hue (local API)** – Smart lighting control over LAN.  
- **TP‑Link Kasa (local API)** – Smart plugs/switches.  
- **Shelly (local API)** – IoT devices via LAN.  

---

## 🎵 Media & Entertainment

- **TMDB (The Movie Database)** – Free movie/TV show metadata.  
- **YouTube Data API** – Video metadata and search (free tier, quota limited).  
- **Plex API** – Local media server control (from earlier notes).  
- **Jellyfin API** – Open‑source media server.  
- **Spotify Web API** – Music control (reduced capabilities for new apps, free tier).  

---

## 🛍️ Shopping & Product Data

- **Walmart API** – Product and inventory data, free tier (developer access).  
- **Amazon Product Advertising API** – Product lookup, free tier, requires approval.  

---

## 🗺️ Location & Transit

- **OpenRouteService** – Free routing, directions, geocoding (non‑commercial).  
- **GTFS / City Transit APIs** – Public transit schedule and route data, query‑only.  

---

## 🧩 Local Integrations (Phase‑2/‑3 Approved)

These are not web APIs but local libraries / OS interfaces – all free and governor‑controlled.

### Speech & Audio
- **Local TTS** – Piper, Coqui TTS, OS TTS engines (e.g., Microsoft Speech, eSpeak).  
- **Local STT** – Whisper (local), Vosk, Coqui STT – offline speech‑to‑text.  

### System & File
- **psutil** – System monitoring (CPU, memory, disk).  
- **GPUtil** – GPU monitoring.  
- **Speedtest CLI** – Network speed testing.  

### OCR & Document Processing
- **Tesseract OCR** – Local optical character recognition, user‑invoked.  

### Bluetooth
- **OS‑level Bluetooth API** – List paired devices, connect/disconnect on user confirmation.  

---

## 🚫 Permanently Excluded (Even If Free)

The following are **never** allowed under Nova’s constitutional model, regardless of API availability:

- Autonomous agents / desktop copilots  
- Background observers or screen watchers  
- Smart‑home automation loops  
- Financial execution APIs (purchasing, trading)  
- Any AI with execution authority  
- Multi‑governor systems
