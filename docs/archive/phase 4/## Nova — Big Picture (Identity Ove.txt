## Nova — Big Picture (Identity Overview)

**Nova is a local intelligence containment system designed to keep AI useful, predictable, and fully under the user’s control.**

It is not built to act on its own.
It is built to ensure intelligence remains a tool — never an independent actor.

---

## Core Idea

Most AI systems are designed to become more autonomous — anticipating needs, offering suggestions, and guiding decisions.

Nova is built on a different principle:

**Intelligence may expand.
Authority must remain contained.**

Nova’s role is to create a boundary around intelligence so it can be used safely, deliberately, and only when invited.

---

## What Nova Is

Nova is:

* **Local** — runs under the user’s control, not a distant service acting independently
* **Quiet by default** — nothing happens unless the user initiates it
* **Predictable** — behavior follows clear, consistent rules
* **Transparent** — no hidden decisions, no silent influence
* **Governed** — intelligence operates within strict limits of authority

It responds when asked, then returns to silence.

---

## What Nova Is Not

Nova is not:

* An autonomous agent
* A digital companion or personality
* A system that tries to anticipate your needs
* A platform that learns your behavior or adapts without permission
* A product designed to capture attention or influence decisions

It does not suggest, persuade, optimize, or guide.
It provides information and actions only when explicitly requested.

---

## The Experience

Nova should feel:

* Calm
* Quiet
* Stable
* Non-intrusive
* Under your control

The intended feeling is:

> “Nothing will happen unless I make it happen.”

It behaves like an instrument — present, available, and reliable — not like a participant in your life.

---

## The Role of the Interface

Nova’s interface exists to **show state, not steer behavior**.

It:

* Displays information
* Reflects the current world or system condition
* Updates quietly without drawing attention
* Never highlights, suggests, or decides what matters

The system presents facts, not priorities.

---

## The Trust Model

Nova earns trust through **limitation**, not capability.

Trust comes from:

* Restraint
* Predictability
* Silence when not needed
* Clear boundaries on what it will and will not do

Its design ensures:

**No initiative.
No hidden activity.
No influence without invitation.**

---

## One-Sentence Identity

**Nova is a local, governed intelligence system that keeps AI useful, contained, and fully under the user’s authority — quiet, predictable, and responsive only when asked.**

---

## The Philosophy

Nova exists to prove a different future for AI:

Intelligence does not need autonomy to be valuable.
Power does not need initiative to be useful.
Trust comes from control, not capability.

Nova is intelligence — **contained, calm, and under your command.**
