## 60 Second Nova Demo

1. Open Nova dashboard.
2. Show Intro and Home so the local-first setup is visible.
3. Ask: `What works today?`
4. Ask: `Can memory authorize actions?`
5. Show Trust receipts.
6. Run a safe local action such as `system status`.
7. Ask: `draft an email to test@example.com about tomorrow's meeting`.
8. Stop at the confirmation boundary unless the tester explicitly approves opening the mail client.
9. Close with: Intelligence is not authority.

## Goal

Help a first-time visitor understand Nova in under one minute.

## Current Demo Notes

- Use local/core proof before advanced connectors.
- Do not lead with email, Shopify, OpenClaw, or background-agent surfaces.
- Show that memory and conversation can help Nova understand, but cannot authorize execution.
- Show receipts as evidence, not permission.
